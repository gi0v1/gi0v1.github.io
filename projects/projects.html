RoboCup
Rescue Line robot

This is the fourth iteration of our robot for the RoboCup Rescue Line competition. Our team has been working on it for around a year and a half. Given that we are just two team members, each one has to work on various aspects. I'm responsible for the mechaical design and for part of the coding. Specifically I'm in charge of the development of a program which has to find some balls inside a small room, using a ML object detection algorithm.
We started developing the robot from scratch and had the opportunity to learn a lot along the way. Not only we understood a lot of concepts regarding robotics, indeed we also learned to how to organize team-working and how to carry out successfully such a complicated projecy.
